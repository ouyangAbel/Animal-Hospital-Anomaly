Patient on Fire Emergency Guide: 60-Second Counter in Animal Hospital Anomaly

Put out burning patients fast with the right counter and learn how the 60-second timer works in Animal Hospital Anomaly.

Emergency Guide
2026-07-01 | 6 min read

The Patient on Fire emergency is one of the most visually alarming crises in Animal Hospital (Anomaly). A burning patient sprints through the hospital corridors trailing flames, and you have exactly 60 seconds to extinguish them before they die. Every second they burn drains -1 sanity/second from everyone in proximity, making this a dual threat: the timer and the sanity drain.

How It Triggers

Patient on Fire can trigger in three ways:

  • Random emergency spawn — Starting Shift 3, this emergency can fire randomly during any shift.
  • Ambulance Event cascade — During the 6+ patient surge, burning patients can appear among the arrivals.
  • Fire-in-Room proximity — A patient standing in a burning room for too long will catch fire themselves, creating a secondary emergency.

The 60-Second Timer

From the moment flames appear on the patient, the clock starts. 60 seconds is tight but manageable if you react immediately. The patient will sprint randomly through the hospital, potentially spreading the emergency to multiple rooms. Do not chase aimlessly — equip your counter item first, then intercept.

Counter Options Ranked

  1. Fire Extinguisher — The one-shot counter. One spray and the fire is out. No sanity cost. Unlimited refills at wall stations. This is the definitive solution.
  2. Ointment — The manual fallback. Applies burn treatment to the patient. Slower than the Fire Extinguisher and consumes the item, but works if you have no extinguisher.

Do not try anything else. Shooting or tasing a burning patient kills them and counts as a failed emergency. Only the Fire Extinguisher or Ointment resolves this correctly.

Sanity Management After Extinguishing

The burning patient drains -1 sanity/second from everyone within a certain radius. After resolving the fire:

  • Check your sanity immediately. If below 40, use Coffee or Chocolate.
  • Check if the fire spawned a secondary Fire-in-Room event in any rooms the burning patient passed through.
  • Scan for fainted patients — the sanity shock can knock low-sanity NPCs into the fainted state.

Can Patient on Fire Be an Anomaly?

Yes. A patient arriving at the window already on fire is likely an anomaly manifestation rather than a random emergency. These patients should be rejected at intake. Check the patient sheet: if the photo shows a normal-looking patient but the live view shows flames, reject immediately.
