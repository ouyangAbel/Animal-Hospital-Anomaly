What is Animal Hospital (Anomaly)?

Animal Hospital (Anomaly) is a Roblox horror experience created by Animal Anomaly where you treat animal patients while watching out for paranormal threats. The game blends medical simulation with anomaly-detection gameplay across 5 escalating shifts.

Every shift you cycle through six steps: check in the next patient, observe their condition, procure the matching item, administer treatment in the correct room, monitor until recovery, then discharge them. But patients aren't always what they seem 闁?anomalies hide among them, and getting it wrong means your shift ends in a wipe.

The game targets horror-roleplay fans aged 13+ who enjoy mechanic-driven survival challenges. With its mix of cute patient designs and creeping dread, Animal Hospital Anomaly appeals to players who want more depth than typical Roblox horror games.

The Core Loop

Your shift breaks into three layers: managing the patient queue, performing diagnoses and treatments using the right items, and spotting anomalies before they harm patients or staff. Each anomaly has unique warning signs and counter-actions 闁?getting them wrong triggers team wipes or jumpscares.

The Three Detection Layers

  • Visual check 闁?Look at the patient directly at the admission window for anatomical oddities (Three Eyes, Hollow Face, Human Teeth, Twitching)
  • Photo Camera 闁?Snap a photo to reveal anomalies invisible to the naked eye (Cursed Photo, Different Eyes, Static Photo, Incorrect Photo)
  • CCTV Monitor 闁?Check the security camera feed for discrepancies (Censored Eyes, Skinwalker, Void Body, Unnatural Body)

Every anomaly in the game is caught by one of these three checks. Skip a layer for any patient and you're betting that the anomaly happens to belong to a class you can still catch.

  2. Learn to Spot Anomalies 闁?Check under beds, compare camera feed vs photo feed, and never look up when lights flicker. Start with Bed Monster and Skinwalker 闁?they appear from Shift 1.
