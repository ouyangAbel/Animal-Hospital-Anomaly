Censored Eyes (Black Eyes)

How to detect and counter the Censored Eyes (Black Eyes) anomaly in Animal Hospital Anomaly. CCTV-only detection and counter-strategy.

CCTV
July 1, 2026

The Censored Eyes anomaly (also known as Black Eyes) is one of the five CCTV-Class anomalies in Animal Hospital Anomaly. It's only detectable on the security camera feed -- the live view at the window shows a normal patient. This guide covers how to detect Censored Eyes and the best counter-strategy.

What Is the Censored Eyes Anomaly?

When you open the CCTV monitor, you'll see the patient's face obstructed by a black rectangular bar across the eyes, similar to a censorship bar. Underneath the bar, realistic staring eyes are visible, creating an unsettling effect. The patient looks completely normal at the admission window -- only the camera reveals the truth.

The black bar is the key visual tell. It's clearly artificial and doesn't match any normal patient rendering. If you see a censorship bar on the CCTV feed, that's Censored Eyes.

How to Detect Censored Eyes

  • Open the CCTV monitor -- This anomaly is ONLY visible on the security camera feed.
  • Look for a black bar -- A rectangular black bar should appear across the patient's eyes.
  • Check for eyes underneath -- Realistic staring eyes should be visible beneath the bar.
  • Compare to the live view -- The patient will look completely normal at the admission window.

Why CCTV Is Essential

Censored Eyes is the strongest argument for always opening the CCTV feed. Without checking the camera, you'll never catch this anomaly. The visual check at the window is completely clean -- the patient looks normal. Only the CCTV monitor reveals the black bar and staring eyes.

Make it a habit to open the CCTV feed for every patient. It takes 2-3 seconds and can save your run. The CCTV check is one of the three mandatory detection layers:

  1. Visual check -- Look at the patient directly
  2. Photo Camera -- Snap a photo to reveal hidden details
  3. CCTV Monitor -- Check the security camera feed

What Happens If You Miss Censored Eyes?

If you admit a patient with the Censored Eyes anomaly, they will transform into a Skinwalker on the hospital floor. The Skinwalker is the most dangerous enemy in the game -- it will attack you and other staff members, dealing bite damage and draining sanity.

Counter-Strategy

The best counter is CCTV detection. When the patient appears at the admission window:

  1. Complete the visual check first
  2. Open the CCTV monitor and look for a black bar across the eyes
  3. If you see the bar, reject the patient immediately
  4. The rejection is clean -- no sanity cost, no consequences

Shift Appearances

Censored Eyes can appear starting in Shift 2 and continues through higher shifts. It's a mid-game anomaly that tests your CCTV discipline.
