Sanity Recovery Tips: Maximize Every Restore

Advanced strategies for keeping your sanity topped off in Animal Hospital (Anomaly). Every restore counts when the hospital is closing in.

Advanced Guide
June 29, 2026 | 10 min read

Our Sanity Management Guide covers the basics —?how sanity drains, what each item does, and the 50% threshold. This article goes deeper: the advanced recovery tricks that separate good runs from great ones.

Chocolate vs Coffee —?The Real Math

Both restore sanity, but they're not interchangeable. Here's the breakdown:

  • Chocolate: 20% per bite —?3 bites = 60% total. Costs more. Best for emergency restores and pre-shift preparation.
  • Coffee: 5% per sip —?3 sips = 15% total. Cheapest option. Best for incremental maintenance between patient batches.

The rule of thumb: use Coffee to maintain, Chocolate to recover. If you're above 60% sanity, Coffee is enough. Below 60%, switch to Chocolate. Below 40%, use Chocolate immediately —?you're in the hallucination danger zone.

The Pre-Shift Sanity Buffer

Before each shift begins, you have a brief window in the lobby. This is the best time to top off sanity:

  1. Buy Chocolate from the Supplies Shop if you have coins. One bar = 60% restore. Start the shift at full sanity.
  2. Talk to Ron from Accounting —?his dialogue sometimes hints at which anomalies are active. Knowing what's coming lets you avoid sanity-draining encounters.
  3. Check your class passive —?Doctor and Surgeon restore sanity passively. If you're playing these classes, you can afford to start slightly below 100%.

Mid-Shift Recovery Windows

The best time to restore sanity is during natural downtime between patient batches. Here's when to use what:

  • Between patient batches (sanity 70-90%) —?One Coffee. Quick, cheap, keeps you comfortable.
  • After anomaly encounter (sanity 50-70%) —?One Coffee or two Chocolate bites. Don't wait —?the next encounter could drop you below 50%.
  • During event downtime (sanity 30-50%) —?Full Chocolate bar. You need the 60% boost to get back to safe territory.
  • Critical (below 30%) —?Chocolate + Anti-Psychotics. The Anti-Psychotics slows decay while Chocolate restores. Buy time for the next recovery window.

Class-Specific Recovery Strategies

Doctor —?The Passive King

Doctor's passive restores 1 sanity per correctly treated patient. In a busy shift with 10+ patients, that's 10+ free sanity. Strategy: maximize patient throughput. Every patient you treat correctly is free sanity. Pair with Extra Check-In Window for double the passive procs.

Surgeon —?The Sustain Machine

Surgeon's passive grants sanity AND speed on every cure. A clean run compounds into a self-sustaining loop —?the more patients you treat, the saner and faster you get. Strategy: avoid damage entirely. The passive only works on successful cures, so don't waste time fighting. Run, treat, repeat.

Psychologist —?High Risk, High Reward

Psychologist doubles ALL sanity events —?gains AND losses. This means Chocolate restores 120% (capped at 100%), but Stalker costs 20 sanity instead of 10. Strategy: play defensively. Your gains are huge when clean, but your losses are catastrophic when messy. Only use Chocolate (never Coffee) because the doubled value makes it overwhelmingly efficient.

Secretary —?The Lobby Grinder

Secretary restores 1 sanity per check-in. In a lobby-heavy build with Extra Check-In Window, you can check in patients rapidly and stack the passive. Strategy: stay at the admission window. Let teammates handle treatment while you grind check-ins for passive sanity.

Sanity Drain Avoidance —?The Free Recovery

The best sanity recovery is not losing sanity in the first place. These habits save more sanity than any item:

  • Never look at Mass of Eyes —?Break line of sight immediately. The drain stops the moment you look away.
  • Avoid the Stalker —?Keep your camera angled toward the floor when transitioning between rooms. Eye contact costs 10 sanity.
  • Lights off in empty rooms —?Every lit room is a small ongoing drain. Two seconds to flip the switch saves sanity over the full shift.
  • Minimize Photo Camera usage —?Each photo costs 10 sanity. Only use it when you suspect an anomaly, not on every patient.
  • Close CCTV quickly —?Camera Figure costs sanity the longer you stay on the feed. Open, scan, close. Don't linger.

The Emergency Restore Kit

When sanity drops below 30% and you're in active danger, this is the emergency sequence:

  1. Chocolate bar —?60% restore. Use immediately.
  2. Anti-Psychotics —?Slows decay rate. Buys time for the Chocolate to take effect.
  3. Retreat to a safe room —?Close the door, turn off lights, wait for sanity to stabilize.
  4. Coffee —?Top off with Coffee while waiting. The incremental 15% adds up.

Do NOT attempt treatment or anomaly detection while below 30% sanity. Hallucinations will cause you to misidentify threats and waste resources.

Sanity Economy —?When to Spend, When to Save

Sanity items are finite. Here's when to spend and when to save:

  • Spend freely above 60% —?You have buffer. Use Coffee for minor dips.
  • Spend carefully at 40-60% —?Switch to Chocolate. Don't waste Coffee on big dips.
  • Hoard below 40% —?Only use Chocolate. Save Coffee for after you've recovered above 50%.
  • Never use Chocolate above 80% —?You're wasting 20-40% of its value. Wait until you need it.
The sanity system rewards players who manage proactively rather than reactively. If you're constantly scrambling to restore sanity, you're already behind. The goal is to never need the emergency kit in the first place.
