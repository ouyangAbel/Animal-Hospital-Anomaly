Death Ritual Emergency Guide: 4 Counter Options in Animal Hospital Anomaly

Cancel the Death Ritual with any of 4 counter methods — ranked from fastest (Taser) to last-resort (candle kicking).

Emergency Guide
2026-07-01 | 7 min read

The Death Ritual is the most dramatic emergency in Animal Hospital (Anomaly). A patient levitates above their bed surrounded by ritual candles, a timer starts ticking, and failure means the patient dies. But unlike other emergencies with a single counter, the Death Ritual gives you four different ways to cancel it — each with different speed, cost, and requirements.

What Is the Death Ritual?

Visually, the patient floats in the air with dark ritual candles arranged around the bed. A timer appears (30-60 seconds depending on source — patches may have changed the duration). If the timer expires without cancellation, the patient dies and you lose significant sanity.

How It Triggers

The Death Ritual has a specific trigger condition: admitting a Hollow Face anomaly. Hollow Face patients have dark hollow eyes, twitching motions, and a hunched posture. If you admit one at the intake window, they will eventually trigger the Death Ritual from their hospital bed. This is 100% preventable — always check for Hollow Face visual tells at intake.

Counter Options Ranked by Efficiency

  1. Tase the patient — No sanity loss. Instant cancel. Requires Taser in inventory. This is the best option if you have a Taser.
  2. Defibrillate — Instant cancel. Requires access to Room 7 (Heart Monitor room). Fastest if you are near Room 7. No sanity cost.
  3. Apply Eye Drops / IV Drops / Coffee — Cancels the ritual on application. Costs the item but no sanity loss. Good middle-ground option.
  4. Kick all candles — 1-3 sanity cost per candle kicked. Must extinguish ALL candles within the timer window. Slowest and most sanity-costly. Last resort.

The Timer Controversy

Community sources disagree on the Death Ritual timer: some report 30 seconds, others 60 seconds. This may vary by patch version. Treat 30 seconds as the safe planning number. That gives you comfortable margin regardless of which version you are playing.

Prevention

Reject Hollow Face patients at the window. Check for dark hollow eyes, twitching body movements, and hunched posture during the visual intake check. A quick CCTV check will also reveal the hollow face feature. Preventing the ritual is always better than countering it.
