Slime

How to counter the Slime environmental hazard in Animal Hospital Anomaly. Mop cleanup, Utility Closet source, and floor hazard avoidance.

Environmental
July 1, 2026

The Slime is an environmental hazard in Animal Hospital Anomaly. A viscous puddle on the floor that damages on contact. This guide covers how to detect Slime, the Mop counter, and where to find the Utility Closet.

What Is the Slime?

Slime is a viscous puddle that appears on the floor of hospital rooms and hallways. It damages you on contact, draining health and potentially sanity. The Slime is an environmental hazard -- it doesn't move or chase you, but it's always dangerous if you walk through it.

How to Detect Slime

  • Look at the floor -- Slime appears as a shiny, viscous puddle.
  • Note the color -- Slime is typically dark green or purple.
  • Avoid walking through it -- Contact causes damage.

The Mop Counter

The primary counter to Slime is the Mop. Hold E to clean the Slime. The Mop is available from the Utility Closet and is free to use.

Where to Find the Mop

The Mop is located in the Utility Closet, which is accessible from the main hallway. The Utility Closet is a free resource -- always grab the Mop when you pass by.

Counter-Strategy

The best counter is immediate Mop cleanup. When you encounter Slime:

  1. Avoid walking through the Slime
  2. Grab the Mop from the Utility Closet
  3. Hold E to clean the Slime
  4. Continue with your normal workflow

Prevention

Slime is an environmental hazard that appears randomly. You can't prevent it from spawning, but you can clean it up quickly to avoid damage. Make the Mop a regular part of your inventory management.

Shift Appearances

Slime can appear starting in Shift 2 and continues through higher shifts. It's a common environmental hazard.
