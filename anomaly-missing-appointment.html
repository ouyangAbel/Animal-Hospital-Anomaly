Missing Appointment

How to detect and counter the Missing Appointment anomaly in Animal Hospital Anomaly. Paperwork detection, appointment list check, and counter-strategy.

Paperwork
July 1, 2026

The Missing Appointment anomaly is one of the two Paperwork-Class anomalies in Animal Hospital Anomaly. It presents as a patient who is in the queue but doesn't appear on the appointment list. This guide covers how to detect Missing Appointment and the best counter-strategy.

What Is the Missing Appointment Anomaly?

In this anomaly, a patient is waiting in the queue but their name doesn't appear on the appointment list. The patient looks normal at the window, the photo is clean, and the CCTV feed shows nothing suspicious. The only way to catch this anomaly is by checking the appointment list against the queued patients.

Missing Appointment is unique because it defeats all three standard detection layers (visual, photo, CCTV). None of them will reveal the anomaly. Only the paperwork check catches it.

How to Detect Missing Appointment

  • Check the appointment list -- Open the appointment list and look for the patient's name.
  • Compare to queued patients -- Every patient in the queue should have a corresponding entry on the list.
  • Look for mismatches -- If a patient is queued but not on the list, that's Missing Appointment.
  • Check every patient -- Don't skip the appointment check, even if the visual/photo/CCTV checks are clean.

Why the Appointment Check Is Essential

Missing Appointment is the strongest argument for always checking the appointment list. Without this check, you'll never catch this anomaly. The visual, photo, and CCTV checks will all come back clean -- the patient looks normal on every layer. Only the paperwork reveals the truth.

Make it a habit to check the appointment list for every patient. It takes 2-3 seconds and can save your run.

What Happens If You Miss Missing Appointment?

If you admit a patient with the Missing Appointment anomaly, they will transform into a Skinwalker on the hospital floor.

Counter-Strategy

The best counter is appointment list verification. When the patient appears at the admission window:

  1. Complete the visual, photo, and CCTV checks
  2. Open the appointment list
  3. Check if the patient's name appears on the list
  4. If the patient is not on the list, reject them immediately
  5. The rejection is clean -- no sanity cost, no consequences

Shift Appearances

Missing Appointment can appear starting in Shift 4 and becomes more common in higher shifts. It's a late-game anomaly that tests your thoroughness.
