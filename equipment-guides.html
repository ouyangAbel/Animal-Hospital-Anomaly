Animal Hospital Anomaly Items & Shop Upgrades Guide

Every item �?what it does, what it counters, where to find it. Plus all 8 permanent shop upgrades with costs and buy priorities.

Items in Animal Hospital Anomaly break down into four functional categories: Treatment (cures a specific condition), Survival (counters a specific enemy or hazard), Tool (utility �?Photo Camera, weapons), and Utility (consumables like Coffee, Speed Cola). The categorization matters because it affects where the item lives in the hospital.

Condition �?Item Treatment Map

Every condition has exactly one correct treatment item. Memorize this table �?it's small and you'll have it down in 10 minutes.

  • Bleeding �?Bandages
  • Bruises �?Medkit
  • Dehydration �?IV Bag
  • Dried Eyes �?Eye Drops
  • Flu �?Cough Syrup
  • Headache �?Medicine
  • High Fever �?Cooling Gel
  • Low Sugar �?Maple Syrup

Permanent Shop Upgrades

8 permanent upgrades available between shifts from the Supplies Shop (unlocks after Shift 3). These persist across run resets and are the primary use of Animal Coins.

Priority 1

50% Faster DNA Analysis

Halves DNA processing time �?9 seconds to 4.5 seconds. $110 Animal Coins. The highest-impact shop purchase according to community consensus. Speeds up the entire patient pipeline.

Priority 2

Extra Check-In Window

Opens a second admission window for simultaneous dual check-ins. $190-200 Animal Coins. Warning: opens a new Head Banger spawn location at the second window.

Priority 3

Patients Recover 25% Faster

Speeds patient post-treatment recovery �?35 seconds to ~26 seconds. $115 Animal Coins. Frees rooms faster for next intake.

Utility

33% Faster Computers

Computer processing time reduced 33% �?2 seconds to ~1.3 seconds. $110 Animal Coins. Applies hospital-wide.

Utility

Faster Check-In

Skip the patient's self-introduction; forms placed immediately. $250 Animal Coins. Reduces per-patient admission time.

Utility

+1 Carry Capacity

Adds 1 inventory slot �?moves you from the 3-slot baseline to 4. $100 Animal Coins. Available post-Shift 3.

Utility

NPC Speed Upgrade

Helper NPCs move faster �?tier 1 is +10% ($150), tier 2 is +12% ($250, requires tier 1). Patients visibly wear blue boots after tier 1.

Advanced

Treat From Inventory

Skip the trip to the medicine cabinet �?apply treatments directly from inventory bar. $450 Animal Coins. DANGER: can administer wrong meds easily. Take only after you trust your slot labelling.
