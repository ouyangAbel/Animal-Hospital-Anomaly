Skinwalker (Shapeshifter)

How to detect and counter the Skinwalker Shapeshifter anomaly in Animal Hospital Anomaly. CCTV detection, prevention strategy, and the most dangerous anomaly in the game.

CCTV
July 1, 2026

The Skinwalker (Shapeshifter) anomaly is the most dangerous anomaly in Animal Hospital Anomaly. It's a CCTV-Class anomaly that appears as a normal patient at the window but reveals its true form on the security camera. Once admitted, it turns hostile and must be fought with weapons. This guide covers how to detect Skinwalker, why it's so dangerous, and how to prevent it.

What Is the Skinwalker Anomaly?

The Skinwalker is a shapeshifting entity that disguises itself as a normal patient. At the admission window, it looks completely innocent -- no visual tells, no strange behavior. But when you open the CCTV monitor, the camera reveals its true form: a distorted, monstrous figure that bears no resemblance to the patient at the window.

The Skinwalker is the most dangerous anomaly in the game because it's the hardest to detect and the most damaging if missed. Every missed anomaly in the game transforms into a Skinwalker on the hospital floor -- but the Skinwalker anomaly itself is a double threat: a missed detection AND a hostile enemy.

How to Detect the Skinwalker Anomaly

  • Open the CCTV monitor -- This anomaly is ONLY visible on the security camera feed.
  • Compare window and monitor views -- The patient should look identical on both. If they don't, it's a Skinwalker.
  • Look for distortion -- The CCTV feed may show the patient's body as distorted, monstrous, or completely different.
  • Check for other CCTV tells -- Skinwalker may also exhibit Void Body, Unnatural Body, or other CCTV anomalies.

Why the Skinwalker Is So Dangerous

The Skinwalker is dangerous for three reasons:

  1. Hard to detect -- It looks normal at the window, so visual checks won't catch it. You MUST check CCTV.
  2. Hostile when admitted -- If missed, it becomes a floor enemy that attacks players, deals bite damage, and drains sanity.
  3. Common conversion -- Every missed anomaly transforms into a Skinwalker, making it the most frequent hostile encounter.

What Happens If You Miss the Skinwalker?

If you admit a Skinwalker patient, they will:

  1. Transform into a hostile Skinwalker enemy on the hospital floor
  2. Attack you and other staff members with bite damage
  3. Drain your sanity continuously while in contact
  4. Require weapons to defeat (Gun, Taser, or Fire Extinguisher)

Counter-Strategy: Always Check CCTV

The best counter is CCTV detection. When the patient appears at the admission window:

  1. Complete the visual check first
  2. Open the CCTV monitor and compare the feed to the live view
  3. Look for any differences between the two views
  4. If the patient looks different on the monitor, reject immediately
  5. The rejection is clean -- no sanity cost, no consequences

Prevention Workflow

The best Skinwalker fight is the one you never have. Prevention workflow at every check-in:

  1. Look at the patient visually -- check for visual anomalies
  2. Snap a photo -- check for photo anomalies
  3. Open CCTV -- check for CCTV anomalies including Skinwalker
  4. Check appointment list -- check for paperwork anomalies

Do all four checks on every patient. Skipping one layer is how Skinwalkers get in.
