Get in Touch

We read every message and do our best to respond within 48 hours. Whether you need help with a specific puzzle, want to suggest a guide topic, or have found an error in one of our walkthroughs, please reach out.

Email Us

For general questions, guide feedback, error reports, and business inquiries, please send us an email at:

contact@animalhospitalanomalyguides.com

When contacting us, please include:

  • Your name (or gaming alias)
  • The specific guide or page your question relates to
  • A clear description of your question or issue
  • For error reports: the platform you are playing on and the game version

What We Can Help With

  • Guide Questions - Stuck on a puzzle? Not sure how to beat a boss? Ask us.
  • Error Reports - Found an inaccuracy in one of our guides? Let us know so we can fix it.
  • Guide Requests - Want a guide for something we have not covered yet? Tell us.
  • Business Inquiries - Partnership opportunities, advertising, and collaboration.
  • General Feedback - Suggestions for improving the site or our guides.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. During periods of high volume or major game updates, response times may be slightly longer. We appreciate your patience.

For urgent matters related to website functionality or technical issues, please include "URGENT" in your email subject line.
