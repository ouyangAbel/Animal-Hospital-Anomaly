Animal Hospital Anomaly Survival & Tool Items Guide: Weapons, Counters & Diagnostics

Every survival tool and weapon explained — Fire Extinguisher, Taser, Gun, and the items that keep you alive.

Items Guide
2026-07-01 | 9 min read

Survival and tool items are what keep you alive when anomalies break through. This category covers weapons, detection tools, and counters — the gear you reach for when a Skinwalker appears or a room catches fire. There are 8 items in this category, ranging from free wall-mounted equipment to class-exclusive weapons.

Fire Extinguisher

The most important survival item in the game. This free pressurized canister handles three different threats:

  • Eliminates Hiders — the anomaly that hides in corners and dark areas
  • Removes Slime puddles — clears the floor hazard that slows movement
  • Extinguishes active fires — both Patient on Fire and Fire-in-Room
  • Eliminates Skinwalkers — the free-to-play counter for the most dangerous enemy

The Fire Extinguisher has 100% charge that depletes with use and recharges at 1% per 2 seconds at its spawn location. Wall-mounted stations are located throughout the hospital hallways. Always carry one from Shift 2 onward. It is free, unlimited, and handles four different threats. There is no reason not to have one.

Photo Camera

The primary anomaly-detection tool. Issued at the admission window from the start of the game. The Photo Camera captures evidence invisible to the live monitor — snap a photo of every patient at intake and compare the photo to the live view. Mismatches reveal anomalies. Costs 10 sanity per photo view, so do not spam it. Use it deliberately: photo at intake, check for discrepancies, move on.

Flashlight

Starter equipment that illuminates dark corners and reveals hidden anomalies under beds. Essential for the Bed Monster detection workflow — shine under every bed after admitting a patient. The Flashlight is always in your inventory from Shift 1 and does not take a slot. Use it reflexively.

Mop

A cleaning implement from the Utility Closet that removes Slime puddles. This is the only weapon that works on Slime — Fire Extinguisher does not clear it, weapons do not affect it. If you see green goo on the floor, get the Mop. Slime slows movement and can trap you during emergencies. Free from the Utility Closet.

Taser

The most reliable reusable weapon in the game. Instantly incapacitates Skinwalkers and other hostiles on contact. Available in two shop variants:

  • 1-use Taser — $150 from the Supplies Shop (Shift 3+)
  • 5-use Taser — $250 from the Supplies Shop (Shift 3+)
  • X-Taser (5-use) — Security class starter equipment

The Taser is also the best Death Ritual cancel option — one zap ends the ritual instantly with zero sanity loss. If you have a Taser in inventory, the Death Ritual becomes a 2-second non-event.

Gun

The most powerful weapon in the game. Ranged, one-shot kill on Skinwalkers and most hostiles. 20 uses per unit. However, there are significant drawbacks:

  • Class-exclusive — only the Secret Agent class has access
  • Robux purchase — not available for in-game currency
  • Friendly-fire risk — shooting near patients or teammates causes them to faint or die
  • Noise effect — the gunshot sound causes nearby NPCs to faint from shock

The community is split: Gun is preferred by paid players for range and reliability; Fire Extinguisher is the free-to-play alternative with more total uses. Never shoot near the intake area.

Smelling Salts

A fast-acting stimulant that instantly wakes fainting patients. Counters both the Fainting Patient anomaly and the Patient Fainted emergency event. This turns a 60-second carry-and-transport into a 2-second instant fix. Relatively affordable from the Supplies Shop ($100-$150). Carry at least one from Shift 3 onward.

Thermo

A diagnostic tool that reads patient body temperature for High Fever confirmation. Diagnostic only — it does not treat anything. Use it when you suspect High Fever but need confirmation before administering Cooling Gel. Standard medical equipment, free to use.
