Animal Hospital Anomaly All Shifts Walkthrough

A complete shift-by-shift guide covering every mechanic, anomaly, and emergency in Animal Hospital (Anomaly). From Shift 1 onboarding to Shift 5 endurance.

This Animal Hospital Anomaly walkthrough covers every shift from the tutorial to the final endurance night. Each section includes anomaly detection workflows, item locations, and survival tips. Use the shift links below to jump to any specific chapter.

Animal Hospital (Anomaly) runs on 5 shifts. Each unlocks new anomalies and tightens the time pressure. After Shift 5 you enter endless survival mode with scaling difficulty. The first two shifts are documented in detail; later shifts introduce progressively harder mechanics.

The Core Loop (Every Shift)

Every shift you cycle through six steps:

  1. Check in the next patient at the front desk. Verify their ID photo against the live camera feed.
  2. Observe them on the HUD: which condition is showing? Dehydration, high fever, something else?
  3. Procure the matching item from the Supply Room shelves or the Med-cart.
  4. Administer the item in the correct room (IV Room for fluids, Surgery Room for stabilization).
  5. Monitor the patient's status gauge until it crosses the "Recovery" threshold.
  6. Discharge them to the exit door.

If the patient is actually an anomaly in disguise �?or if an anomaly manifests in the room while you're working �?you drop the workflow and respond to the threat.

Shift 1 �?Onboarding

Difficulty: Tutorial | Active Threats: Bed Monster, Skinwalker (low frequency) | Reward: $63 fixed

What Happens

Dr. Harlow runs the briefing dialogue and walks you through the basic flow: check in, verify photo, look under the bed, apply Maple Syrup. Two scripted patients teach you the treatment workflow (Herbs + Eye Drops). Shutters and cameras unlock at completion.

Anomalies Active

  • Bed Monster �?appears under patient beds. Always check under every bed after admitting a patient. Counter: Maple Syrup.
  • Skinwalker �?low frequency. Discrepancy between camera and photo means reject. Counter: Fire Extinguisher if admitted.

Key Items to Find

  • Herbs �?spawns outside the relevant treatment room (for Stomach Ache)
  • Eye Drops �?spawns outside the relevant treatment room (for Dried Eyes)
  • Maple Syrup �?critical for Bed Monster encounters
  • Photo Camera �?issued at the admission window from the start

Unlock Requirement for Shift 2

5 successful discharges with no major incident.

Pro Tip: Shift 1 is your tutorial. Don't rush �?practice the detection workflow (visual check �?photo �?CCTV) on every patient. This muscle memory pays off from Shift 3 onward.

Shift 2 �?Infrastructure Glitches

Difficulty: Easy-Medium | Active Threats: Bed Monster, Mass of Eyes, Skinwalker | New Mechanic: Ceiling threats

What Changes

Tutorial scripting ends. The ceiling becomes hostile with the Mass of Eyes. You start needing Eye Drops in your active slot. Sanity monitoring starts to matter �?see our Sanity Management tips.

New Anomalies

  • Mass of Eyes �?writhing cluster of eyes on the ceiling. Drains sanity while you look at it. Counter: Eye Drops or keep camera angled down. NEVER look up.
  • Head Banger �?appears at the check-in window, smashing its head. Counter: Coffee or food.

Key Survival Tips

  • Always carry Eye Drops once you unlock Shift 2
  • Use the security monitor to sweep halls before leaving the office
  • Start managing Sanity �?Chocolate and Coffee are your restore items
  • The Barney NPC arc begins after Shift 2 �?pay attention to his requests

Unlock Requirement for Shift 3

8 minutes of game time with sanity above 50%.

Shift 3 �?First Real Difficulty Spike

Difficulty: Medium | New Features: Supplies Shop, Emergency Wing, Timed Emergencies | Active Threats: All previous + Slime Ooze

What Changes

This is where the game gets real. Three major systems unlock simultaneously:

  • Supplies Shop opens �?run by Shopkeeper Nurse. Buy Coffee, Speed Cola, Taser, and more with Animal Coins.
  • Emergency Wing accessible �?Rooms 6, 7, 8 with minigame-based treatment (X-Ray sequence, Heart Monitor, Surgery timer).
  • Timed emergencies begin �?Fire in Room, Patient Fainted, Death Ritual, and more can fire at random.

New Threats

  • Slime Ooze �?environmental hazard on floors. Counter: Mop (free from Utility Closet).
  • Emergency Events �?timed crises that interrupt your workflow. Each has its own clock and counter.

Room 8 �?Where Runs Die

Room 8 (Surgery) has the highest failure density: 45-second timer, randomised tool layout, and cross-hazard susceptibility. Walk in knowing the tool you need. Improvising during the 45-second window is how patients die.

Pro Tip: If you're stuck in the Shift 1-3 loop, the bottleneck is usually inventory + class progression rather than skill. Save coins, unlock Surgeon or Security, then push to Shift 4.

Shift 4 �?Ambulance Event Debut

Difficulty: Hard | New Mechanic: Ambulance Event | Active Threats: All previous + multi-anomaly stacking

What Changes

Shift 4 is the consensus difficulty jump. Three mechanics activate simultaneously:

  • Ambulance Event �?6+ patients arrive near-simultaneously. Dr. Harlow announces an anomaly attack. Triage decisions matter for the first time.
  • Paperwork-class anomalies enforceable �?Missing Appointment and Mimic Patient (which copies Ratthew) are now active.
  • Skinwalker spawn frequency climbs �?partly because new anomaly types can convert.

Ambulance Triage Order

  1. Burning patients first �?60s timer is the tightest
  2. Critical Patient timers �?100-120s but needs full treatment chain
  3. Skinwalker conversions from any anomaly admitted in the panic
  4. Stable admissions �?last

Pre-Shift 4 Loadout

  • Fire Extinguisher �?handles Patient on Fire and Fire-in-Room
  • Maple Syrup �?handles Bed Monster events
  • Coffee �?sanity restore between events
  • Large Speed Cola (if affordable) �?transit between cascading events

Shift 5 �?Final Core Shift

Difficulty: Very Hard | Key Feature: Head Banger peak density | Completion: Standard Discharge ending

What Changes

The final numbered shift. Successful completion triggers the Standard Discharge ending and the Professional Caretaker achievement. Head Banger reaches peak spawn density. All anomaly types and emergency events are active.

Survival Strategy

  • By now you should have Surgeon or Security class unlocked
  • Carry a full combat loadout: Fire Extinguisher + Taser + Maple Syrup + Coffee
  • Use the detection workflow on EVERY patient �?no shortcuts
  • Manage Sanity aggressively �?Chocolate is 4x more efficient than Coffee
  • Complete the Barney storyline for your preferred ending

After Shift 5

The game enters endless survival mode with scaling difficulty. Difficulty keeps scaling past Shift 10 �?there is no cap. Documented runs go higher; most players don't. Community difficulty bands: Shifts 1-3 (learning), 4-6 (mid-game), 7-9 (veteran), 10+ (endurance).

What Persists Between Shifts

Persists

  • Animal Coin balance
  • Class unlocks (permanent)
  • Shop Upgrade purchases (permanent)
  • Shift number progression

Resets Each Shift

  • Inventory consumables (unless class starts with fixed items)
  • Sanity meter
  • Active patients on the floor
  • Anomaly queue
