Sanity Management Guide: How to Keep Your Sanity Above 50%

How sanity drains, what each restoration item actually does, and the rotation pattern that keeps you above 50% all shift.

Mechanics Guide
June 25, 2026 | 9 min read

Sanity ignorance is the single biggest cause of Shift 2 wipes. You can master the anomaly detection workflow, carry the right items, and still lose a run because you let your sanity drop below critical levels without realizing it.

How Sanity Drains

Sanity is a shared global resource — every player in the shift draws from the same pool. It drains from multiple sources simultaneously:

  • Environmental exposure — being near active anomalies (Mass of Eyes, Ghost, Stalker) drains sanity per second while you're in range or looking at them.
  • Photo Camera usage — every photo view costs 10 sanity. Cursed Photos cost additional sanity on top of that.
  • Room lighting — every lit empty room is a small ongoing drain. Lights off in empty rooms is free sanity preservation.
  • Event timers — Fire in Room drains -1 sanity/second while burning. Death Ritual drains sanity while candles are active.
  • Direct encounters — Stalker costs 10 sanity on eye contact. Camera Figure costs sanity the longer you stay on CCTV.

Sanity Restoration Items — What Actually Works

Not all sanity items are created equal. Here's the real data:

  • Chocolate — 20% per bite, 3 bites per bar, 60% total recovery. The most efficient sanity item in the game. 4x more potent per use than Coffee.
  • Coffee — 5% per sip, 3 sips per cup, 15% total recovery. Cheapest and most accessible. Also ends Head Banger encounters.
  • Anti-Psychotics — Slows sanity decay rate, not an instant restore. Maintenance item for high-anomaly shifts. Best used proactively, not reactively.
  • Doctor class passive — Every correctly treated patient restores 1 sanity. Scales with patient throughput.
  • Surgeon class passive — Each successful cure grants sanity AND a speed boost. The strongest sustain in the game.

The 50% Threshold

Below 50% sanity, the game starts introducing hallucinations — false anomaly indicators that can cause you to reject legitimate patients or miss real threats. The Psychologist class doubles both gains AND losses, making this threshold even more dangerous.

Your goal every shift: never let sanity drop below 50%. Here's the rotation:

  1. Start of shift — Use Chocolate immediately if sanity is below 80%.
  2. Mid-shift — Coffee between patient batches. One cup = 15% restore.
  3. During events — Chocolate for emergency restore. Coffee is too slow during active crises.
  4. Between shifts — Chocolate from the Supplies Shop if available.

Class-Specific Sanity Tips

  • Doctor — Your passive restore means you can survive longer without consumables. Focus on patient throughput to maximize the passive.
  • Psychologist — Double-edged sword. In a clean run, the doubled gains compound beautifully. In a messy run, the doubled losses are catastrophic. Pick this only when you're confident in damage avoidance.
  • Surgeon — Every cure = sanity + speed. A clean run compounds into a self-sustaining loop. The strongest endgame class for sanity management.
  • Secretary — Sanity per check-in. Lobby-heavy playstyles scale the passive linearly. Pair with Extra Check-In Window upgrade for maximum value.
The single most important sanity rule: don't look at things that drain sanity. Mass of Eyes, Stalker, Camera Figure — all of them punish observation. Break visual contact immediately and the drain stops.
